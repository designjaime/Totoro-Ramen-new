OUR STORY

Our story begins when we first fell in love with the animated movie about a playful and massive cuddly creature known as Totoro. It is here that Hee Choi introduces a concept that sparked the inspiration to bring her ramen for customers to pleasantly experience a magical adventures with our naturally brewed bone broth.

Our main goal is to offer an enjoyable experience for our customers by providing new self-ordering system using iPads and best possible services together as well as extremely rich and flavorful ramen.

The Totoro ramen possesses a delectable taste of natural pork bone broth which is both extremely rich and flavorful. The secret lies within the rich taste and flavor of the meticulously prepared pork broth base soup and the melted super tender pork back fat as a final flourish to the ramen.

The rich flavored soup is cooked with pork bones after more than 18 hours of boiling. For the first 10 hours, the cauldron is boiled with the strong fire to break bones into flakes. Then, it is simmered for another 10 hours to extract the genuine pork bone broth. This process makes the soup rich and tasty. You will feel the deepness of our broth after a sip of it. Our pork broth, cooked fresh daily for 18-20 hours. With this delicious broth married with noodles cooked al dente, you will have a ramen bowl so good you want to come back for seconds!

Miso Ramen

Shoyu Ramen

Veggie Shoyu Ramen

Soy sauce flavored ramen, veggie broth, corn, fresh green onion, bean sprout, bamboo shoots, kikurage mushroom, thin red pepper, sesame seed, fried tofu. (ask for half cut soft boiled egg to add or not)

Curry Ramen

Curry flavored ramen, fresh green onion, bean sprout, bamboo shoots, kikurage mushroom, corn, soft boiled egg, chicken katsu.

Niku Ramen

Spicy chilli miso flavored ramen, pork broth, spicy pork, fresh green onion, bean sprout, roasted pork, bamboo shoots, kikurage mushroom, half cut soft boiled egg, roasted seaweed.

Tonkotsu Ramen

Maje Ramen

Soup-less ramen, fresh green onion, bean sprout, bamboo shoots, kikurage mushroom, diced chashu, fried diced garlic, thin cut roasted seaweed, poached egg, corn.
